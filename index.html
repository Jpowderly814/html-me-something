My Trip to Santorini

In August 2015, I was able to take a trip to Santorini which has been my favorite vacation.We stayed for 10 days, traveling around the small island after first stopping in Athens for a day.We were able to stay near the beach, near the cliffside, and finally in the largest city, Fira.

Favorite Things to Do

  1. Sailing
  2. Winery
  3. Hiking
  4. Kayaking
  5. Beach

Sailing

One evening, we embarked on a sunset sail where we enjoed food, drinks, snorkeling, swimming, and a beautiful view of the sunset.

Santorini Sailing

Winery

Santorini has many wineries to chose from, but we chose to visit one of the larger wineries:Santo. There was an amazing view of the volcano and we chose a wine tasting that include a "sampling" of twelve(!) different wines for only 20 €.

Santos Winery

Hiking

We were able to go hiking several times during our trip to Santorini. Sometimes it was on purpose and other times it was because the transportation never arrived (the buses don't neccessarily stop for you). Our second day there we went for a hike up to ancient Thira.
